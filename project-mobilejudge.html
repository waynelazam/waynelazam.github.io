UniCriJS Mobile

  • Description: An application which is built to solve the prevailing manual tabulation or manual computation of scores in a competition or in an event (singing, dancing, beauty pageant, etc.). Its core purpose is to make the life of the event organizers, our target client, easy. It reduces the use of towering paperwork that might compromise the integrity of the data inputted and the corresponding results. With so much textual information to enter, entering of data can inevitably make mistakes that can have big and sometimes disproportionate consequence.
  • Tech Stack: Android Native, Java, Gitlab, Android Studio
